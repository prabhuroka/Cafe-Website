Biography

Prabhu Roka's entrepreneurial journey traces back to his formative years, where he honed his skills and passion for hospitality in the bustling restaurant scene of Houston, Texas. From a young age, Prabhu immersed himself in the intricacies of culinary arts and customer service, working at local eateries where he learned the importance of quality, consistency, and attention to detail.

Upon entering the University of Houston, Prabhu recognized an opportunity to channel his love for food and socialising into a venture that would resonate with his fellow students. Drawing upon his diverse restaurant experience and his innate knack for bringing people together, Prabhu founded Cafe UH, a cosy yet vibrant establishment nestled in the heart of the university campus.

Moreover, Prabhu remains committed to giving back to the community by supporting local charities and initiatives through fundraising campaigns and donation drives.As the visionary behind Cafe UH, Prabhu remains steadfast in his commitment to innovation, customer satisfaction, and community enrichment. With his entrepreneurial spirit, culinary expertise, and passion for bringing people together, he is poised to continue expanding his presence in the university market while leaving a lasting impact on campus culture.
